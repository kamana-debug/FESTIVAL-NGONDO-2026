A Word from the President

Anticipation and Vigilance

Sawa brothers and sisters, NGONDO 2026 invites us to uphold the values that have shaped our identity for centuries. This edition's theme — Anticipation and Vigilance — is not a slogan: it is a call to collective action.

Building a collective awareness of today's challenges, preserving and passing on the Sawa cultural heritage to the rising generations, strengthening community unity: these are the commitments we make together.

May the Wouri River continue to carry our messages of hope and unity. Jajongwanele o Kotome.

H.M. Mbodi Épée Gaston
Executive President of NGONDO 2026 · Superior Chief of Bassa Canton

The 13 Participating Cantons

1
Bassa du Wouri Canton
H.M. Mbodi Épée Gaston
Superior Chief · President of NGONDO 2026

First settlers of the Wouri riverbanks alongside the Bakoko. The largest of the Wouri cantons — 23 villages, 75% of the department.

🏛️ Presidency 2026
2
Bèlè Bèlè Canton
H.M. Paul Milord Mbappe Bwanga
Superior Chief of Bèlè Bèlè Canton

Founded by Bèlè ba Doo, who crossed the Wouri. Historic bearer of the KIEL'A NGONDO vision.

🔱 Superior Chief
3
Deïdo Canton
H.M. Ekwalla Essaka Frédéric James
Superior Chief of Deïdo Canton

Canton of the ancestor Ebele, who set off in search of salt. Five lineages: Bonajinge, Bonateki, Bonatene, Bonamoudourou, Bonantone.

🌊 People of the Water
4
Bonambela (Akwa) Canton
H.M. Dr Louis Din Dika Akwa
King Akwa · Superior Chief

Kingdom of the legendary King Dika Mpondo. 20 villages under the emblem of the Eagle. Mbela Mbengue and Mbela Jedu.

🦅 The Royal Eagle
5
Njo Njo (Bell) Canton
H.M. Jean-Yves Eboumbou Douala Manga Bell
King Bell · Superior Chief

Canton of great resistance. Rudolf Duala Manga Bell, hanged on August 8, 1914, a symbol of Sawa heroism against the occupier.

⚔️ Historic Resistance
6
Bonkeng-Penja Canton
H.M. Dissake Dieudonné
Superior Chief · 2nd Vice-President of NGONDO

Fertile lands of Mount Cameroon. Birthplace of Penja Pepper PGI. Three communities — Bonkeng, Bafun and Abo.

🌶️ Penja Pepper PGI
7
Bodiman Canton
H.M. Ngalle Edouard
Superior Chief of Bodiman Canton

Descendants of Mbondo II Male known as "Dima". Guardians of the forest of 51 sacred baobabs — Koro Mabuma — in Mandimba.

🌳 51 Sacred Baobabs
8
Bakoko Wouri Canton
H.M. Songue Madiba Erick Jamil
Superior Chief of Bakoko Wouri Canton

Clan that arrived on the banks of the Wouri in the 16th century. Guardians of the Bissima (water deities). Home of Japoma Stadium.

🏟️ Japoma Stadium
9
Wouri Bwélé Canton
H.M. Edimo Alexandre Martin
Superior Chief of Wouri Bwélé Canton

237 km², 16 villages. The 2026 Caravan stopped here on June 28 — arriving by the waters of the Wouri, with wrestling and Eyombwè.

🌊 2026 Caravan
10
Wouri Bosua Canton
H.M. Superior Chief of Wouri Bosua
Superior Chief of Wouri Bosua Canton

A people in communion with the Miengu, water spirits. The Ngondo's best paddlers — regular winners of the race.

🛶 Masters of the River
11
Bakoko-Moungo Canton
H.M. Njoke Essane Njoke
Superior Chief of Bakoko-Moungo Canton

A Bakoko community established even before contact with the Germans in 1885. A tradition of community justice going back centuries.

⚖️ Ancestral Justice
12
Malimba Canton
H.M. Me Matanda Eugene Jacques
Superior Chief · Malimba Edéa Canton

From the mouth of the Sanaga to Edéa. Exclusive specialty: behona (clams). Four families: Bongo, Mulimbe Mbengue, Mulimbe Jedu, Mulongo.

🦪 Behona — Clams
13
Elong Canton
H.M. Ehodé Mbébi Michel
Superior Chief of Elong Canton

A canton of the Sawa people, rich in history and ancestral traditions passed down along the Wouri River.

🌿 Sawa Tradition

Sawa Tradition

The Grand Pirogue Race

A test of endurance on the Wouri — hand-carved giant pirogues, steered by dozens of paddlers to the rhythm of drums. The paddlers of Wouri Bosua are said to be in communion with the Miengu.

Every canton fields its team in this competition that is far more than a sport: an affirmation of identity and a bond with the sacred river.
